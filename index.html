About Us

At Chargcom, we are dedicated to powering the future of transportation by providing reliable, efficient, and sustainable EV charger installation solutions. Based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in installing a wide range of electric vehicle charging stations for homes, businesses, and public spaces. Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles by making charging infrastructure easily accessible and hassle-free for everyone.

Our Mission

To empower the shift to electric mobility by providing comprehensive EV charging solutions that are accessible, affordable, and environmentally responsible.

Our Vision

A future where electric vehicle charging is as seamless and ubiquitous as fueling a traditional vehicle, driving the world towards a cleaner, greener tomorrow.

Our Services

  • Residential EV Charger Installation: Whether you're a homeowner looking to install a charger or upgrade your current setup, we offer customized solutions that fit your needs and budget.
  • Commercial EV Charger Installation: We partner with businesses, property managers, and developers to design and implement scalable EV charging solutions, creating a future-proof environment for EV users.
  • Public Charging Stations: We assist local governments and private entities in installing public charging stations, supporting the growing demand for accessible EV infrastructure.
