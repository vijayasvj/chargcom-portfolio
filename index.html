About Us

At Chargcom, we are dedicated to powering the future of transportation by providing reliable, efficient, and sustainable EV charger installation solutions. Based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in installing a wide range of electric vehicle charging stations for homes, businesses, and public spaces. Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles by making charging infrastructure easily accessible and hassle-free for everyone.

Our Mission

Our Vision
